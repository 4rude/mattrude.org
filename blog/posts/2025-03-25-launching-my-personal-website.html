Launching My Personal Website

March 28, 2025

Today marks an exciting milestone. I have finally launched my personal website!

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on Cloudflare's infrastructure, this entire site costs less than $8 per year to maintain. A comparable WordPress site might cost $200/year including domain and security certificates, plus additional fees for plugins and ongoing maintenance. This cost disparity reflects a pattern I have observed throughout my career as a software developer and data engineer: the significant gap between vendor pricing and actual implementation costs.

This technology affordability gap scales dramatically:

  • Small organizations often spend hundreds annually on basic websites with limited customization
  • Mid-tier businesses invest thousands in proprietary systems while using just a fraction of their features
  • Enterprises commit to huge contracts for capabilities that could be built for a fraction of the cost

One organization I worked with saved $2400 annually and improved data reliability by replacing their existing synchronization process with a custom solution accelerated by AI tools. This exemplifies how modern development approaches can make specialized solutions more accessible than ever.

My Mission: Democratizing Technology

My vision is to bridge this gap by challenging two common assumptions: that powerful technology requires enterprise-level budgets, and that implementing custom solutions requires specialized expertise. The evidence increasingly suggests neither is true.

I am creating practical resources that empower everyone, regardless of technical background or budget constraints, to implement robust technology systems by leveraging:

  • Affordable infrastructure with generous free tiers (like Cloudflare)
  • Open source solutions that rival commercial offerings
  • Clean, simple code patterns that are easier to maintain
  • Secure design principles that protect data without specialized knowledge
  • AI-assisted development tools that amplify basic technical skills

This approach creates better, more tailored solutions that precisely match your needs rather than forcing your processes to conform to off-the-shelf software limitations.

What to Expect

To put these principles into action, I'm developing a series of practical resources that progress from fundamental to advanced implementations. Here's what you can look forward to in the coming weeks and months:

  • Practical Applications of AI Tools for Non-Technical Users
  • Essential Cybersecurity Practices for Small and Medium Organizations
  • Custom Survey and Data Collection Systems
  • Lightweight CRM Solutions for Small Organizations
  • Integration Strategies for Existing Business Tools
  • Data Visualization Tools and Dashboards

These tutorials won't assume extensive coding experience—if you can follow instructions and have basic computer literacy, you can implement these solutions. I will emphasize how modern code patterns combined with AI tools can make development accessible to non-specialists while creating technology that truly serves everyone's needs.

While these resources will empower many readers to build their own solutions, I recognize that some situations call for more direct collaboration. That's why I am also offering several ways we can work together...
